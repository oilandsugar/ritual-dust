This page is a collections of notes, ramblings and highlights for each year I pass through. I'm starting this at the end of 2021 with the intention of adding to it yearly. A good companion to this is the [Timeline](/timeline) which retraces my projects and noteschronologically.

{{< spacer >}}

## Highlights of 2025

- Rescued 3 baby squirrels who lost their mother, nursed them for a while and then passed them on to other people for them to be released in the wild
- Got married with Hex and got matching ogham tattoos for it
- Did lights for my first trance show/rave at SAT and met some of my favorite artists in the process  
- Filmed our most elaborate music video yet for [Suffer Puppet](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AFtZH23leUg) with Voidxwitch, which included a custom-built set, VFX, dolly shots, a custom video game, and much more
- Finished putting together a circuit bent analog video glitcher and used it to make a [music video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z57_xGY6Huc)  
- Finished [the map of the North](https://piousrot.com/north/) for Pious Rot
- Started putting together a starter kit and adventure for Pious Rot
- Wrote and recorded 8 edm-adjacent songs for my upcoming album under the name L1Zb3th, some of which will have vocals by me.
- Illustrated by first book cover
- Kept challenging myself with tattooing, doing some bigger pieces, lettering and precising my style
- Designed and illustrated the full physical release of the new Aura Merlin album "Moon Gates", which was the first vinyl package I did full design and illustration for
- Re-kindled my love for 3d modelling and got my blender skills up to date
- Dyed some of my hair green, started electrolysis
