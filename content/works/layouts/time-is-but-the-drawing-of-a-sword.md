Design for the tape release of "Time is but the drawing of a sword", by [Lyndsie Alguire](https://www.lyndsiealguire.ca/music). The album is an epic journey through emotional landscapes and internal worlds.

The cover of the album is a beautiful painting by [Naomi Nowak](https://www.instagram.com/naominowak/).
