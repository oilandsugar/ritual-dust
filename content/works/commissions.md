## Sliding scale

I offer sliding scale pricing based on your financial situation, the two prices matching with the two levels of the sliding scale. Note that these prices are a guideline, depending on the specifics of the project, the planned usage, the amount of work and detail these can vary.

For every item on this page the price on the left is the full price and the price on the right in parentheses is the reduced price.  

full price ---> <span class="accent">100$ (50$)</span> <--- reduced price

Here are some guidelines to help you pick the price that is best based on your situation

<div class="row accent mobile-vertical">
    
<div class="flex-1 smaller-padding-left smaller-padding-right">
<h3>Full price</h3>
<ul>
    <li>I am confortable enough to meet all my basic needs</li>
    <li>I may have some debt but it doesn't prevent me from meeting my basic needs</li>
    <li>I have expandable income</li>
    <li>I have access to financial savings</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="flex-1 smaller-padding-left smaller-padding-right">
<h3>Reduced price</h3>
<ul>
    <li>I frequently stress about meeting my basic needs</li>
    <li>I have debt that sometimes prohibits me from meeting my basic needs</li>
    <li>I have very limited expandable income</li>
    <li>I have little or no savings</li>
</ul>
</div>
    
</div>

I also extend a genuine invitation for BIPOC clients & trans folks to request lower rates, in recogition of structural wealth inequality created by white supremacy & transphobia.
