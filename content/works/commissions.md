I am happy to work wth bands, artists, writers, publishers, event organizers and small businesses for projects of small size (like the artwork for an upcoming album) or larger size (multiple illustrations and layout for a publication).

I can create work suitable for editorial, publishing, merch, packaging and website projects and provide the appropriate digital files for printing if need be.
